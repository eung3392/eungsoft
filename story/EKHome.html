개발 배경: "왜 만들었나요?"

로키드 글래스를 처음 접하면 수많은 앱을 설치하게 됩니다. 하지만 정작 자주 쓰는 앱은 5~6개 내외임에도 불구하고, 기본 시스템 UI에서는 원하는 앱을 찾기 위해 여러 번의 조작이 필요했습니다. 특히 시스템 홈 화면을 변경할 수 없다는 제약은 더욱 큰 불편함으로 다가왔습니다.

EKHome은 이러한 답답함을 해결하기 위해 "사용은 터치로, 설정은 마우스로"라는 원칙 아래 기획되었습니다. 가장 필요한 기능만 남기고 나머지는 과감히 걷어낸, 진정한 미니멀리즘 런처입니다.

Introducing the most simple and intuitive Favorites-based Home Launcher for Rokid Glasses users.

rocket_launch

Background: "Why was it created?"

When you first get your Rokid Glasses, you tend to install numerous apps. However, despite having many apps, the ones you actually use frequently are limited to about 5 or 6. In the default system UI, finding these apps required multiple steps, which was inconvenient. The restriction of not being able to change the system home screen made this even more frustrating.

EKHome was conceived to solve this problem under the principle: "Usage via touch, Settings via mouse." It is a true minimalist launcher that retains only the most essential features and boldly removes the rest.

Key Design Points

blur_on

Simple & Clean Design

We focused on the clock and basic status information, excluding complex elements. It aims for a neat layout that minimizes eye strain in AR environments.

ads_click

Sticky Interactions

By implementing subtle scale animations when touching or dragging widgets, we've created a 'sticky' feedback system that lets you physically feel what you are controlling.

animation

Fluid Spatial Transitions

The widgets scatter and regroup with graceful Ease In-Out motions when opening and closing the app drawer, making the screen transitions feel almost alive.

build_circle Core Feature Guide

1

Free Widget Placement (Drag & Drop)

Place various widgets like clock, battery, notes, and calendar anywhere on the desktop. Even if you restart the app, it perfectly remembers your custom layout.

2

Bottom Favorites Bar (Quick Dock)

Choose only the apps you actually use and put them in the bottom bar. This is the optimal location for the quickest execution within the AR glasses' narrow field of view.

3

Clean Status Monitoring

Check vital system information like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and battery in real-time through widgets. Readability is maximized through the harmony of text and icons.
